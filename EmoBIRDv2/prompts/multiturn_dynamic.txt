If you prefer to avoid docetaxel entirely, alternatives include T-DM1 (20-30% alopecia rate) or starting with trastuzumab + pertuzumab alone (minimal hair loss). Your oncologist can review which option balances cancer control with your quality-of-life priorities—keeping you looking and feeling like yourself for your grandchildren." [156 words]
